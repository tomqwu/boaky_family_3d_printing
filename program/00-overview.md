# Boaky Family Summer 3D Printing Program — Overview

**Eight weeks, one Bambu Lab H2C, three makers.** July 6 – August 30, 2026.
This is the master schedule; everything else in [`program/`](.) hangs off it.

## The goal

Start of summer: we can download models and print them in multiple colors.
End of summer: **each of us has designed, printed, and published original work** —
Peter an original city district, Matt an original tabletop soccer game, and Dad a
family of makers who can run every capability this printer has.

| Maker | Track | Capstone |
|---|---|---|
| **Peter (13)** | [City Studio](02-peter-city-studio.md) — buildings, real city data, Fusion 360 | Original multi-plate city district |
| **Matt (10)** | [Toy Lab](03-matt-toy-lab.md) — toys, mechanisms, TPU | Original tabletop soccer game |

Everyone does the shared **skills ladder** in [01-h2c-mastery.md](01-h2c-mastery.md);
the tracks are where each person points those skills at what they love.

## The weekly rhythm (camp mode, ~7–9 hrs)

| Day | What | Time |
|---|---|---|
| **Mon** | Skill session — this week's technique from the [skills ladder](01-h2c-mastery.md) | 60–90 min |
| **Wed** | Track session — Peter and Matt work their own tracks | 60–90 min |
| **Fri** | Track session #2 + queue up weekend prints | 60–90 min |
| **Sat** | Big build session — the week's main project, together | 2–3 hrs |
| **Sun** | Show-and-tell: what worked, what failed, log it, pick next week's prints | 30 min |

Long prints run between sessions. **Dad approves anything unattended or overnight.**
No plan survives contact with an exciting idea — the schedule is a menu, not
homework. Off-plan excitement wins; grab something from the
[idea bank](05-idea-bank.md) any day.

## Standing rules

1. **Safety:** the nozzle hits 350°C and the bed 120°C. Nothing comes off the plate
   until the bed cools. Hands out of the chamber while the gantry moves. Repairs,
   nozzle work, and anything with blades or solvents = Dad. Details in
   [08-troubleshooting.md](08-troubleshooting.md).
2. **Failures are data.** Every failed print gets a photo and a line in the
   [build log](../logs/) before it goes in the scrap bin. Stuck? Ask Claude —
   see [How to Ask Claude](../qa/HOW-TO-ASK-CLAUDE.md).
3. **Original beats copied.** Downloading is how we learn; designing is why we're
   here. Every week from week 3 on, everyone makes at least one thing that didn't
   exist before.
4. **Publishing goes through Dad's account** (MakerWorld is 13+ with guardian
   consent, 18+ for anything monetized). Pre-publish family review: good photos,
   no personal info.
5. **Sunday is sacred.** The 30-minute show-and-tell is the one non-negotiable
   session — it's where the summer compounds.
